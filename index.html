About Our Company

A Legacy Built on Stone & Trust

In just half a decade, Sai Ganga Granites and Minerals has carved a strong and trusted name in India's natural stone industry. Rooted in quality and driven by craftsmanship, we source, process, and deliver the finest granite varieties across the country and beyond.

Founded with a vision to bring nature's timeless beauty to every home and structure, our quarries and processing units ensure that every slab leaving our facility meets the highest standards of quality, finish, and durability.

Direct Quarry to Consumer No middlemen — better prices, guaranteed freshness
Custom Cutting & Polishing Precision fabrication to your exact specifications
Pan-India Delivery Network Timely delivery with safe packaging guaranteed
